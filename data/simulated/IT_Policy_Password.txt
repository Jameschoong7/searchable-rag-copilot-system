1. PURPOSE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
This policy defines the rules for creating, managing, and resetting
employee account passwords across all Centific Malaysia systems. It
applies to all full-time employees, contractors, and interns with
access to company systems.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
2. PASSWORD REQUIREMENTS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
All employee passwords must meet the following minimum requirements:

- Minimum length: 12 characters
- Must contain at least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- Must contain at least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- Must contain at least one digit (0-9)
- Must contain at least one special character (e.g. !, @, #, $)
- Must NOT contain the employee's username or full name
- Must NOT reuse any of the last 10 passwords

Passwords expire every 90 days. Employees will receive a reminder
email 14 days before expiry.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
3. SELF-SERVICE PASSWORD RESET
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Employees can reset their own password without contacting IT Helpdesk
by following these steps:

Step 1: Navigate to the Microsoft Self-Service Password Reset portal
        at: https://aka.ms/sspr

Step 2: Enter your corporate email address
        (e.g. yourname@centific.com)

Step 3: Select your preferred verification method:
        - Option A: Approve via the Microsoft Authenticator app
          (recommended)
        - Option B: Receive a one-time code via registered mobile
          number

Step 4: Follow the on-screen instructions to set a new password that
        meets the requirements in Section 2.

Step 5: Log in to your workstation using the new password within
        15 minutes. The session token expires after 15 minutes.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
4. FULL LOCKOUT PROCEDURE (MFA DEVICE LOST)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
If you have lost your MFA device and cannot complete the self-service
reset, follow this escalation procedure:

Step 1: Contact the IT Helpdesk immediately via:
        - Internal phone extension: 1800
        - Email: ithelpdesk@centific.com
        - Walk-in: Level 3, IT Support Counter (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM)

Step 2: Provide your Employee ID and line manager's name for identity
        verification.

Step 3: IT Helpdesk will issue a temporary password valid for 24
        hours. You must change it immediately upon first login.

Step 4: Re-register your MFA device via the Microsoft Authenticator
        app within 24 hours of account restoration.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
5. ACCOUNT LOCKOUT POLICY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Accounts are automatically locked after 5 consecutive failed login
attempts. The lockout duration is 30 minutes. After 30 minutes, the
account will automatically unlock. If repeated lockouts occur, the IT
Helpdesk will be notified automatically.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
6. POLICY COMPLIANCE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Failure to comply with this policy may result in account suspension
and disciplinary action in accordance with the Centific Malaysia
Employee Code of Conduct. All access logs are monitored by the
Security Operations team.
